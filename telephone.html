the telephone

take a message, leave a message

1. Pick up the handset, listen for instructions.

2. Listen to the last message recorded

Please be aware, the phone isn't monitored or censored, parents listen once before you let your kids.

3.1 Dial "0" to record your own message

Respond to previous, share what's on your mind, talk about a loved one, etc., etc.
Please refrain from making deragatory or demeaning comments, no one needs a record of you being cruel.
Please do not include any identifying information. Strangers are gonna hear this.

3.2 Alternatively, dial "3" to listen to a randomized series of prior messages.

Dialing "0" at any point allows you to record, making yours the most recent message someone will hear next.
